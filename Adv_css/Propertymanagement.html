Build Ventures: Property Management

Tenant Screening & Placement

Managed by: Bebe Carloto Property + Maintenance Manager

Finding the right tenants is crucial for a successful rental experience. Our thorough tenant screening process includes background checks, credit reports, rental history verification, and income assessments to ensure we place responsible and financially stable tenants in your property. We also conduct in-depth interviews and reference checks to gauge reliability and ensure long-term tenancy. By selecting the best-qualified renters, we help minimize risks such as late payments, property damage, or early lease terminations.

Lease Management & Legal Compliance

Managed by: Bebe Carloto Property + Maintenance Manager

Managing lease agreements and ensuring compliance with local, state, and federal rental laws can be complex, but we handle it all with precision. By staying up to date with the latest housing laws and regulations, we protect your property and investment from potential legal issues.
