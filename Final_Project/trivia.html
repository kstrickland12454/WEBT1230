Fun Facts & Trivia

  • Original Name: The show was originally called Whoopass Stew! before becoming The Powerpuff Girls.
  • Hidden Face: Miss Bellum’s face was never fully shown to emphasize intelligence over appearance.
  • Mojo Jojo’s Speech: His long-winded monologues were inspired by classic anime villains.
  • Him’s Inspiration: The eerie villain Him was based on horror movie villains and the devil.
  • Celebrity Cameos: Ringo Starr appeared in a 2014 special episode.
  • Beatles Tribute: The episode Meet the Beat-Alls was filled with Beatles references.
  • Fourth Wall Breaks: The show frequently had characters acknowledge they were in a cartoon.
  • Emmy Award Winner: The series won multiple Emmy Awards for its creativity.
  • Banned Episode: See Me, Feel Me, Gnomey was initially banned in the U.S. due to controversial themes.
  • Massive Merchandising: The franchise expanded to toys, clothing, and even McDonald's Happy Meal toys.
