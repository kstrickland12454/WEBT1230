Lady Knockouts Softball

The Lady Knockouts is an 8U girls' softball team based in Harrison, TN. Now in their second year in the league, the team is dedicated to fostering a love for the game while promoting teamwork, growth, and fun. Comprised of talented and enthusiastic young players, the Lady Knockouts focus on building skills, forming strong bonds, and creating unforgettable memories both on and off the field. This close-knit team is passionate about competition, sportsmanship, and celebrating the joy of softball at every step of their journey.

Code of Conduct

The Sports Code of Conduct sets clear guidelines to uphold ethical standards, ensure a safe environment, and foster respect, integrity, and fairness in sports. Our mission is to develop skills, strength, agility, and character—both on and off the field. As we strive for success, we expect every player, parent, and coach to demonstrate leadership, respect, and sportsmanship at all times.

  • Support and Encourage: Show respect to all participants, regardless of their skill level, and foster a positive and inclusive environment.
  • Positive Environment: Maintain zero tolerance for misconduct and focus on finding constructive resolutions to challenges.
  • Respect for Coaches & Officials: Demonstrate appropriate behavior at all times, including during games, practices, and social events.
  • Sportsmanship: Embrace victories with humility and handle losses with grace.
